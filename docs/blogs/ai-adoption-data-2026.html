India ke paas duniya ka doosra sabse bada internet user base hai — 800 million+. Lekin AI adoption abhi Global South average (14%) ke aas paas hai. Kyu? Language barrier, awareness ki kami, aur yeh belief ki "AI sirf English mein kaam karta hai." Lekin ab Hindi mein AI available hai — yeh sab badalne wala hai.

🤔 Yeh Data Kya Kehta Hai?
🎯
Opportunity bahut badi hai

6.8 billion log abhi AI se bahar hain. Jaise 2010 mein smartphone ka boom aaya — waise 2026-2030 mein AI boom aane wala hai. Jo log aaj seekh rahe hain woh aage rahenge.

💡
Language ek badi barrier hai

Zyada AI tools English mein hain. Jaise hi Hindi, Tamil, Telugu mein AI better hoga — India ka AI adoption rate rocket ki tarah uthega. Khan Academy ka Hindi content iska ek example hai.

💼
Job market badal raha hai

Jo 0.04% log AI se coding kar rahe hain — unka kaam 10x fast hai. 5 saal mein AI skills jo aaj "extra" hain woh "basic requirement" ban jaayengi.

🌐
Digital divide barhega?

Global North mein 24.7% log AI use karte hain, Global South mein sirf 14.1%. Yeh gap agar badhta raha toh countries ke beech inequality aur barhegi. Isliye India mein AI education zaruri hai.
